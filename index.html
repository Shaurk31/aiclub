AI Pioneers

AI Pioneers is a high school club at Cupertino High School that introduces students to the exciting world of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. We believe in hands-on experimenting and innovation. Our club aims to inspire and educate students in AI and machine learning by providing an engaging, hands-on learning environment. We believe that the future of technology lies in AI and machine learning, and that it's important to equip students with the skills and knowledge that they need to thrive in this exciting field.
